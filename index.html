Juris-M aims to be the last research manager you will ever need. It solves two difficult problems in is a bid to build a research support platform suitable for all fields of study, including legal research and scholarship that crosses language boundaries. The system is made up of several components: a Desktop Client (Firefox plugin) derived from the Zotero research platform; an Abbreviation Filter (Firefox plugin) for style-specific abbreviation support; and the Juris-M extended version of the Citation Style Language.

Legal Styles

Juris-M enables modular application of legal styles, to permit precise, jurisdiction-specific generation of references. To create or modify a jurisdiction module, use the Style Manager provided by this site.
