Juris-M Support

There are several channels of support.

Mailing Lists

If you use Juris-M to support your research work, subscribing to on of the following mailing lists is highly recommended. The lists are the primary channel through which we disseminate news of new releases and critical bug reports.

jurism-announce

Just the essentials.

jurism-support

Suitable for experienced users who want to be informed of problems others experience.

jurism-freshers
New users are encouraged to subscribe to this list. (Students in the Academic Writing program in the Nagoya faculty are added to this list automatically.)

FAQ

Before posting a question to the lists, please check the video FAQ—your question may already have been answered.
